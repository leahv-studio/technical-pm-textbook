# References: Product Management Foundations

1. **[Product Management - Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Product_management)**
    Comprehensive overview of product management as a discipline, covering the role's history, core responsibilities, and organizational positioning. Provides foundational context for understanding the PM function.

2. **[Product Lifecycle - Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Product_lifecycle)**
    Describes the stages a product passes through from introduction to decline, including growth and maturity phases. Essential for understanding how PMs guide products over time.

3. **[Stakeholder Management - Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stakeholder_management)**
    Explains frameworks for identifying, analyzing, and engaging stakeholders across an organization. Directly relevant to how PMs balance competing priorities from cross-functional teams.

4. **Cagan, Marty. *Inspired: How to Create Tech Products Customers Love*. 2nd Edition, Wiley, 2018.**
    Defines modern product management practices at top technology companies, covering product discovery, vision, and team empowerment. A foundational text for any aspiring or practicing product manager.

5. **Banfield, Richard, Martin Eriksson, and Nate Walkingshaw. *Product Leadership: How Top Product Managers Launch Awesome Products and Build Successful Teams*. O'Reilly Media, 2017.**
    Explores leadership strategies for product managers, including building product vision, roadmaps, and high-performing cross-functional teams. Offers practical frameworks for stakeholder alignment.

6. **[What Is Product Management? - Product Plan](https://www.productplan.com/learn/what-is-product-management/)**
    A practitioner-oriented guide that breaks down core PM responsibilities, including strategy, roadmapping, and feature prioritization. Useful as a quick-start reference for newcomers to the role.

7. **[The Product Manager Role - Mind the Product](https://www.mindtheproduct.com/what-exactly-is-a-product-manager/)**
    Explores the often-ambiguous product manager role with real-world examples and community insights. Clarifies how PMs operate at the intersection of business, technology, and user experience.

8. **[Product Strategy Overview - Silicon Valley Product Group](https://www.svpg.com/product-strategy-overview/)**
    Marty Cagan's concise explanation of product strategy, including vision, objectives, and discovery. Provides a framework for aligning product decisions with business outcomes.

9. **[How to Write a Value Proposition - Harvard Business Review](https://hbr.org/2023/02/how-to-write-a-value-proposition)**
    Explains how to craft compelling value propositions that resonate with target customers and differentiate from competitors. Critical skill for PMs defining product positioning and market fit.

10. **[Competitive Analysis Framework - Atlassian](https://www.atlassian.com/work-management/strategic-planning/competitive-analysis)**
    Step-by-step guide to conducting competitive analysis, including identifying competitors, evaluating strengths and weaknesses, and applying findings. Directly supports market research activities PMs perform regularly.
